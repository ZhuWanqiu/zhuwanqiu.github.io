The project so far

Sorry for the delay…

This weekend I just finished the (probably) final story sharing. It’s about me. Spending one and half day outside is not really healthy to me. I feel even more dizzy than Saturday……

It’s more like a documentry than before. The cameraman kept shooting my weekend life while I explained and shared several stories to the audience. I shared why I came here, my amature swimmer life, and showed my favorite places in New York City.

And I am still on the editing of the second video. I guess I spent too much time on editing and I really need to stop chasing perfection and just release what I have so far…

The creaziness of the final kind of freaks me out…
