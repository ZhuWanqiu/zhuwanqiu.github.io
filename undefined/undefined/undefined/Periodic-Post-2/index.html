How’s my project going

I launched the first demo video on Youtube channel.

It’s a simple video about me and why I want to collect your moments of life. I hope that video can help other understand me and why I want to do this project.

I spent all most all the week on video editing because there are so many various materials and I wanna make it as short and funky as possible.
